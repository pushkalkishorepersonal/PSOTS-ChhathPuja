This app saves data in your browser — here's exactly what and why

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. Everything is stored locally in your browser's storage (localStorage) so the app works across visits without a server login. Nothing leaves your device unless you submit a form.

  • Login session — your name, email & Google ID, so you stay signed in
  • Your profile — flat number, mobile, family details — pre-fills your payment form & helps match UPI transactions
  • Contribution history — cached for 10 minutes so pages load faster; refreshed from our Google Sheet automatically
  • Notification preferences — your WhatsApp update choices, remembered between visits
  • Message history — your past messages to the committee, stored locally as a reference
  • App preferences — reminder snooze, returning-user flag — purely for your convenience

You can clear all this at any time: use Delete My Data in My Profile, or clear your browser's site data for this page.
