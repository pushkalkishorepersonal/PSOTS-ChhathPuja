🔒 Data Privacy & Security

✓ Safe for community use

Your data is stored securely on Google's servers with HTTPS encryption on all connections. We use the same infrastructure as Google Forms / Google Sheets — a standard used by millions of community organisations.

Only committee admins can access your full profile (name, flat, mobile). The public contributor list shows only name, flat, and amount.

When you create a profile, we collect:

  • Name, flat/unit number
  • WhatsApp mobile number
  • Email address (from Google login)
  • Vrati status (yes/no)
  • Contribution details (amount, payment method, date)

We do not collect bank details, UPI PIN, payment credentials, or any other sensitive financial data.

Only committee admins can see your mobile number and email. The public-facing contributors list and homepage never display your contact details.

🌅 About the Event

Chhath Puja is an ancient Hindu festival dedicated to Surya Dev (Sun God) and Chhathi Maiya. It is one of the most sacred festivals of Bihar, Jharkhand, and Eastern UP, observed over four days with fasting, bathing in holy water, and offering Arghya (water offering) to the setting and rising sun.

PSOTS has been celebrating Chhath Puja since 2022, and 2026 marks our grand Panch Varsh Mahotsav (5th year celebration).

The four days of Chhath Puja 2026 at PSOTS are:

  • Nov 1 — Nahay Khay (नहाय खाय)
  • Nov 2 — Kharna (खरना) · Prasad at 7:00 PM, Community Hall
  • Nov 3 — Sandhya Arghya (संध्या अर्घ्य) · 5:45 PM, Society Ghat
  • Nov 4 — Usha Arghya (उषा अर्घ्य) · 6:15 AM, Society Ghat
