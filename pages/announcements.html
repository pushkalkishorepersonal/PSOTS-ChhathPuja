पंच वर्ष महोत्सव — 5th Year Grand Celebration! 🌅
This year marks a historic milestone — 5 years of Chhath Puja at Prestige Song of the South! We enter this landmark year with a strong foundation and have set our grandest budget yet for 2026. Expect bigger ghat decorations, extended cultural programme, live bhajans by a professional group, and prasad for all 200+ families.

The committee sincerely thanks each and every contributor and volunteer from the past 4 years who made this journey possible. जय छठी मैया!

Two Community Prasad Events — Open to All Residents
This year we are organising prasad for all PSOTS residents on both the key nights:

🍚 Nov 2 — खरना प्रसाद (Dinner) at the Community Hall, from 7:00 PM onwards. खीर, चावल, और सब्जी — traditional Chhath prasad for the whole community.

🙏 Nov 4 — ठेकुआ प्रसाद distributed at the Society Ghat after Usha Arghya (approx. 6:30 AM). Take home the blessings of Chhathi Maiya.

Volunteers Needed — Ghat Decoration & Setup
We need enthusiastic volunteers for ghat decoration and lighting setup on November 2nd (from 8:00 AM). This is a community effort that truly makes our ghat beautiful. Tasks include arranging marigold garlands, setting up diyas, managing lighting cables, and general setup.

Breakfast will be provided for all volunteers. It's a fun morning — bring your family!
