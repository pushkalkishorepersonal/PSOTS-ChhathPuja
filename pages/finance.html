🙏 Our Transparency Commitment

Every rupee contributed by PSOTS residents is accounted for in full. Expense records are maintained by the committee treasurer and are available for review by any resident on request. We carry forward surplus year to year — no funds are ever returned or redirected. The carry-forward of ₹1,04,670 from 2025 is the seed for our 5th-year grand celebration. We will publish detailed 2026 expense records on this page within 2 weeks after the event.
