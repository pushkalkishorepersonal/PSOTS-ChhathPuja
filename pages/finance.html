🌅 Why ₹6,00,000 this year?

Five years ago, 58 families gathered at our ghat and lit the first diya together. What started as a quiet act of faith has grown into something none of us quite expected — and this year, we want to honour that journey the way it deserves.

🏛️
A Ghat Worthy of 400+ Devotees
Arghya attendance has grown from a few dozen to 400+ devotees. The ghat setup, lighting, and safety infrastructure must grow with the devotion that fills it.
🙏
Prasad for Every Family at PSOTS
We have 2,300+ flats in our society. This year, we want Thekua and Kharna prasad to reach every single door — because no neighbour should feel left outside this celebration.
🎭
A Cultural Programme
Folk dance, children's performances, and cultural skits — Chhath is not just a puja, it is a celebration of who we are and the roots we carry with us.

🙏 Our Transparency Commitment

Every rupee contributed by PSOTS residents is accounted for in full. Expense records are maintained by the committee treasurer and are available for review by any resident on request. We carry forward surplus year to year — no funds are ever returned or redirected. The carry-forward of ₹1,04,670 from 2025 is the seed for our 5th-year grand celebration. We will publish detailed 2026 expense records on this page within 2 weeks after the event.
